2021 HIGHLIGHTS

The Ghana Digital Innovations Week commenced last year, 2021, with a week long series of events and activities to:

  • Celebrate the accomplishments of Ghana’s digital innovation ecosystem 
  • Build momentum towards a common vision for its future development and
  • Mobilize diverse stakeholders towards collaborative and inclusive ecosystem growth.

It was convened by His Excellency the Vice-President of Ghana and other key ecosystem actors and enablers as part of a trilateral program to to celebrate the accomplishments of Ghana’s digital innovation ecosystem and build momentum towards a common vision for its future development and was convened.
